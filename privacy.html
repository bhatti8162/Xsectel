Our Privacy Commitment

At XSECTEL, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We are committed to protecting your information while delivering trusted cybersecurity and GRC consulting services. This policy explains how we handle your data responsibly.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, company, and billing information when you contact us or use our services. We also collect limited technical data like IP address and browser type for analytics and website improvement.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and enhance our cybersecurity and compliance services.
  • To communicate updates, reports, and security advisories.
  • To process secure payments and maintain client records.
  • To meet regulatory, legal, and audit requirements.

Payment Security

All online transactions are processed through secure third-party gateways compliant with PCI-DSS standards. XSECTEL does not store or access your payment card information.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance website performance and personalize your experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site features may not work properly.

Data Protection

We use encryption, access controls, and continuous monitoring to ensure your data remains protected from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Your data is only shared with trusted third parties or authorities when legally required.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Policy Updates

XSECTEL may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new regulations or business practices. The latest version will always be available on this page.
